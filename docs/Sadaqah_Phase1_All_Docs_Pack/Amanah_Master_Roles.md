# Amanah Platform --- Master Roles & Registration Governance

Version: 1.0\
Status: LOCKED BASELINE

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Core Governance Principles

## 1.1 Separation of Duties (Critical Rule)

The platform enforces strict separation between: - Organisation Users:
Operate NGOs and mosques - Platform Users: Evaluate, certify and govern
NGOs

One account cannot be both.\
A single human may hold both roles using separate accounts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.2 Multi-Organisation Access

One user can: - Join multiple organisations - Hold different roles per
organisation - Own multiple organisations

All memberships are logged and auditable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.3 Event & Audit Trail Requirement

Every role action must be: - Timestamped - Traceable - Revocable -
Linked to inviter

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Account Types

account_type: - ORG_USER - PLATFORM_USER

One account_type per account. Cannot be both.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Organisation Roles (amanahOS)

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Role                                Responsibilities
  ----------------------------------- -----------------------------------
  Org Owner                           Register organisation, manage
                                      subscription, assign admins

  Org Admin                           Manage users and settings

  Finance Manager                     Financial reporting & closing

  Finance Staff                       Transactions & receipts

  Program Manager                     Projects & impact

  Compliance Officer                  Policies & disclosures

  Board Member                        Approvals & governance

  Internal Auditor                    Internal reviews

  Staff / Volunteer                   Limited operations

  External Accountant                 Bookkeeping & financial prep
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Platform Roles (Amanah Authority)

  Role                    Responsibilities
  ----------------------- -----------------------
  Super Admin             Full platform control
  Trust Analyst           Analyse trust scores
  Certification Officer   Manage certification
  Shariah Reviewer        Shariah compliance
  External Auditor        Financial audits
  Risk Investigator       Fraud investigations
  Marketplace Manager     Manage professionals
  Support Officer         Onboarding support

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Registration Flows

## Organisations

Self-register via amanahOS and undergo verification.

## Organisation Users

Invited by Org Owner/Admin.

## Platform Users

Created by Super Admin.

## Professionals

Apply → Verified → Invited by organisations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Conflict of Interest Rules

System prevents auditors/reviewers from evaluating organisations they
are linked to.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Governance Summary

-   One account cannot be both org & platform.
-   One human may hold both via separate accounts.
-   Users may join multiple organisations.
-   Freelancers may serve multiple organisations.
-   All actions are audited.
