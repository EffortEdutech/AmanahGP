## 1) Purpose
CTCF is a standardized certification framework evaluating **governance, transparency, traceability, impact discipline, and Shariah governance controls**.

CTCF is **not**:
- a judgement of niyyah/spiritual merit
- a guarantee of impact

---

## 2) Scoring Model (100 points)
**Layer 1: Legal & Governance Compliance** is a **Pass/Fail Gate**. If failed → **Not Certifiable**.

| Layer | Theme | Points |
|---|---:|---:|
| 2 | Financial Transparency | 20 |
| 3 | Project Transparency & Traceability | 25 |
| 4 | Impact & Sustainability | 20 |
| 5 | Shariah Governance | 15 |
|  | **Total** | **100** |

**Certification threshold:** ≥55/100  
**Layer 2 minimum:** ≥12/20 (normalized)

---

## 3) Grades
| Score | Grade |
|---:|---|
| 85–100 | Platinum Amanah |
| 70–84 | Gold Amanah |
| 55–69 | Silver Amanah |
| <55 | Not Certified |

---

## 4) Applicability & Normalization
If a criterion is not applicable (e.g., zakat segregation for a non-zakat org), exclude it from denominator and normalize.

`normalized_layer_score = (earned_points / max_applicable_points) * layer_max_points`

---

## 5) Layer 1 — Gate (Pass/Fail)
All must pass:
- legal identity/registration evidence
- governing document (constitution/bylaws)
- named board/committee/trustees
- conflict of interest policy
- org bank account separation (no personal collection accounts)
- clear contact + physical address proof

Fail any item → Not Certifiable.

---

## 6) Layer 2 — Financial Transparency (20)
(5 pts each; normalize if N/A)
- annual financial statement provided
- audit evidence (external or credible equivalent)
- program vs admin breakdown disclosed
- zakat segregation & traceability (if zakat)

---

## 7) Layer 3 — Project Transparency (25)
(5 pts each)
- project budget vs actual tracking
- geo-verified reporting (geo or verifiable location marker)
- before/after documentation (where applicable)
- beneficiary impact metrics with context
- completion report timeliness

---

## 8) Layer 4 — Impact & Sustainability (20)
(5 pts each)
- KPIs defined
- sustainability/maintenance plan
- continuity tracking cadence (jariah continuity)
- impact-per-cost efficiency metric

---

## 9) Layer 5 — Shariah Governance (15)
- named Shariah advisor (5)
- written Shariah compliance policy (3)
- zakat eligibility governance (3 if zakat)
- waqf asset governance (4 if waqf)
